About

We are Bastion, a team of college students who are sick of ISPs and service-providers taking our data and profiting off of it.
We install a proxy between your client browser and ISPs, preventing companies from taking your data and using it.
Instead, we'll take your data, process it, and sell it for you, giving you a cut of the profit as well. Might as well make some bucks with your data!
